Who We Are

Born From
The Scene

Oz Soundz Records was established as part of The Oz Soundz Group — a family-founded, musician-driven organisation based in Sydney, NSW. Founded by Jason "Plummo" Plumridge and his family, the group has deep, decades-long roots in the Australian grassroots music community.

We're not a corporate label. We're not here to take your masters and ghost you. Oz Soundz Records is being built to be the label we always wished existed when we were coming up through the scene.

A subsidiary of Vestinex Pty Ltd (ABN: 45 147 744 858), Oz Soundz Records is 100% Australian owned and operated — and proudly intends to stay that way.

"Whilst new in the market, The Oz Soundz Group has connection to the Music & Audio Industry at the 'grass roots local level' for more than 4 decades."

— Jason "Plummo" Plumridge, Founder

The Family

Our founder studied music at Newcastle Conservatorium. His eldest son is an accomplished keyboardist — his band released an EP in 2024. His daughter is a committed singer. His youngest son plays trombone and drums. Music isn't the business at Oz Soundz. It's the whole point.

Royalties & Deals

No tricks. No traps. Here's exactly how we structure our deals.

Our Philosophy

Your Music.
Your Masters.

The major label model is broken. Oz Soundz Records is being built on an entirely different foundation — deal structures that respect artists, protect their creative work, and ensure they share meaningfully in the success they create.

Specific deal terms will be negotiated individually with each artist. Our exact royalty splits and deal structures will be published publicly before we open applications in 2030.

"This won't be a label that takes everything — it'll be a partnership built on fairness and genuine belief in the artists we work with."

— Oz Soundz Records

📜Artist Retains Masters

By default, artists retain ownership of their master recordings. Any co-ownership arrangement will be explicit, negotiated, and fair.

💸Transparent Royalty Splits

Our royalty percentages will be published publicly before launch. No hidden deductions. No cross-collateralisation without full artist understanding.

🚪Exit Rights

Artists have the right to exit their deal under defined circumstances. We're not interested in trapping people — if it's not working, we part ways with dignity.

✅Lawyer Up — We Encourage It

We actively encourage every artist to have an independent music lawyer review any deal we offer. Informed artists make better partners.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know about Oz Soundz Records, answered honestly.

When can I apply?

Applications open in 2030. Register your interest now via our contact page and you'll be first to know when the door opens.

Do you charge artists to be signed?

Absolutely not. We will never charge an artist a sign-on fee. Any so-called label that charges you to be signed is not a label — walk away.

What genres do you accept?

All genres. We care about authenticity, passion, and craft — not whether you fit a commercial format. Rock, folk, hip-hop, electronic, country, metal — bring it all.

Do I need to be based in a major city?

No. Australia is full of incredible music outside Sydney and Melbourne. We actively want to represent artists from regional and rural communities.

Will I keep my masters?

By default, yes. We believe artists should own their work. Any arrangement involving shared master ownership will be explicitly negotiated and fully transparent.

Can I be signed if I already have music out?

Yes. Previous releases don't disqualify you. We're interested in where you're going, not just where you've been.

What if I already have a manager or booking agent?

Great. We work with existing artist teams. Label support and management aren't mutually exclusive — we prefer artists who already have professional support around them.

Can unsigned artists access the Oz Soundz community?

Yes! You don't need to be signed. Artist spotlights, the gig calendar, blog features, and sponsorship applications are open to all indie artists.

How is Oz Soundz Records different from a distributor?

A distributor just gets your music on platforms. We provide artist development, branding, press, community building, and genuine career support alongside distribution.

What's the Oz Soundz Group?

Oz Soundz Records is one arm of The Oz Soundz Group — a connected ecosystem including Oz Soundz Music & Audio (gear retail) and Oz Soundz Studios (recording studio, coming 2027/28).

Still have questions? We'd love to hear from you.
